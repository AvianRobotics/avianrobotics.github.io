PATROL SURVEILLANCE WITHOUT BLIND SPOTS

Avian Robotics develops drone security systems for public safety and security applications to reduce emergency response time and minimize risk to human life.

Features





Complete remote surveillance solution

A complete cloud-connected surveillance solution for your business using drones to monitor and patrol your property.



Autonomous flight patrol

Our indoor autopilot allows drones to autonomously fly both pre-programmed and randomized routes for full coverage.



React quickly to emergencies

Intrusions are automatically detected and notifications are delivered through the cloud to your devices so you can respond quickly.



Effective security for less

Our solution will reduce the costs of setting up cameras and deploying officers while reducing response times and increasing coverage.





HD video streaming and surveillance

Capture or view up to 1080p quality video live from your drones with no latency over a secure connection through the internet.



Remote control over the internet

The drone uses your existing Wi-Fi to provide manual control using a web browser or mobile device for teleoperated control.



Integrates with your security infrastructure

Avian’s drones can work with your existing security infrastructure and can be easily integrated with current systems.



Cost effective and easily customizable

Parts can be easily customized, upgraded and replaced using Avian’s 3D printed manufacturing process.
